FLORAL & LAUREL

At F&L, we always offer high-quality products from sustainable sources, and practice sustainability wherever possible. That's why we also offer products made with flowers that may be a couple days old - but still beautiful - at half price. We keep the planet (and your wallet) top of mind at Floral & Laurel.

Check out our 'Specialties' tab for these products (as well as soaps, lip gloss, and more!) that have been made with sustainability in mind. Click on 'Arrangements' to purchase fresh, made-to-order, sustainably-sourced bouquets.
