I think Green Geeks would be the best company for a new, small business. The cheapest plan first caught my attention as it explicitly states "perfect for small websites or blogs that are just getting started." I was comparing namecheap's lowest-price plan, and Green Geeks' low price plan offers only 1 website while namecheap's offers 3 websites. However, I noticed they have different renewal prices for their membership, and namecheap's is nearly 4 times the renewal price for GreenGeeks after the first year. I wouldn't know if my business would scale hugely in its first year and since other features are similar between the two, I would pick Green Geeks (which is also eco-friendly!)
